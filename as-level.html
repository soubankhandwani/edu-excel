The AS Level (Advanced Subsidiary Level) forms the foundation of the full A-Level course. It requires deep conceptual understanding, strong analytical skills, and effective exam strategies.

Key Benefits of AS Level Tuition:
Focused one-on-one attention
Reinforcement of complex topics in Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and Economics
Structured exam preparation with past papers and mock tests
Flexible scheduling to accommodate school and self-study

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between AS Level and A Level tuition?

AS Level covers the first half of the A-Level course. AS Level tuition focuses on core foundational concepts, while A-Level tuition covers advanced applications.

Can I get an AS Level Maths tutor online?

Absolutely. Many platforms offer expert AS Level Maths tutors online, with flexible scheduling, one-on-one lessons, and past paper practice.

How much does AS Level tuition cost?

Fees vary based on subject and tutor experience. On average, AS Level tuition ranges from ₹700 to ₹2,000 per hour in India, and £25–£60 per hour in the UK.

Which board syllabi do AS Level tutors follow?

Most AS & A Levels tutors support Edexcel, Cambridge (CAIE), OCR, and AQA. Always verify their familiarity with your exam board.
