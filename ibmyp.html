About Us

IB-MYP

Welcome to the International Baccalaureate Middle Years Programme (IB-MYP) examination overview. The IB-MYP is an educational framework designed for students aged 11 to 16, focusing on their holistic development and academic growth. At the culmination of this program, students undergo a comprehensive assessment process to showcase their understanding, skills, and knowledge across various subjects and interdisciplinary themes.

The IB-MYP examination assesses students' abilities to apply critical thinking, research skills, and problem-solving techniques in real-world contexts. Through a combination of internal and external assessments, students demonstrate their proficiency in subject-specific knowledge while highlighting their interdisciplinary understanding.
