About Us

IBDP

The International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) is a rigorous, two-year pre-university course of study designed for highly motivated and intellectually curious students aged 16 to 19. The IBDP is known for its holistic approach to education, combining a challenging academic curriculum with a strong emphasis on personal development, creativity, and community engagement.
